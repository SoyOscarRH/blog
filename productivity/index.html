Productivity.

1.
What is the task we are doing? Make sure you are focused on only one thing at a time.
2.
You don't feel like starting? Surprise! It is always like this; motion creates emotion.
3.
Do you have distractions under control? Close the door; close facebook, youtube, and twitter; put your phone on do not disturb; put paper or something equivalent to your side in case you have an idea.
4.
Do you want music? Maybe the best thing is to put white noise, listen to a forest on a rainy day, classical music, or that soundtrack you love so much.
5.
Wait... why are we doing this? Do those tasks that motivate you, never forget the reasons why you do it, remember all the benefits.
6.
What are the parts of this task? Split it into little chunks that are concise ; quantize your work.
7.
Are you bored? Find the way of injecting passion to this task, make it like a game, make it excited.
8.
How are you going to reward yourself when you finish? A smile, shouting victory, that feeling of understanding a little bit more about the world, or a quick 15 minutes break; everything counts.
9.
What is a reasonable goal to accomplish in this session? Put your head on the ground and be realistic with your expectations.
10.
How long are you going to work until taking a real break? Place a maximum time, a specific goal and stick to it, you deserve it.
